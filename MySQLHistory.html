MySQL

 MySQL was created by a Swedish company, MySQL AB, founded by Swedes David Axmark, 
            Allan Larsson and Finnish Michael "Monty" Widenius. Original development of MySQL by 
            Widenius and Axmark began in 1994. The first version of MySQL appeared on 23 May 1995.
